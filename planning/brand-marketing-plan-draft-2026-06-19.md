## 1. Executive summary

Vibe Coders Academy is a **practitioner cohort for developers who already code** and want to ship real projects using **Cursor, MCP, and automations** — not a no-code “vibe coding for beginners” program.

**Market gap:** SoftUni and others own beginner/no-code AI app builders (Bolt, Lovable, Replit). We own **agentic engineering for working devs** in Bulgaria and remote-friendly cohorts.

**Near-term goal (90 days):** Establish credible founder-led brand, fill first cohort waitlist, support PO/consultant hiring with a consistent story, and activate Facebook + LinkedIn without overpromising dates or pricing.

## 2. Positioning & secret sauce

### Positioning statement (draft)

> For **developers in Bulgaria (and remote-friendly EU)** who already ship code but struggle to **systematize AI agents** in their workflow, **Vibe Coders Academy** is a **cohort-based academy** that delivers a **shipped capstone repo, automation pack, and launch artifact** — unlike **generic AI courses or no-code vibe programs**, we teach **Cursor-first agent engineering** with real rules, skills, MCP, and production habits.

### Secret sauce (3 pillars — Denis to confirm)

| Pillar | What it means | Proof we can show |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Ship, don’t slide** | Every week ends in a repo artifact, not theory | Capstone + weekly demos |
| **Agent ops stack** | PM / research / business / social automations as curriculum | This HQ repo + Cursor automations |
| **Practitioner founder** | Denis builds in public; students see real tradeoffs | LinkedIn build logs, live Q&A |

### Who it’s NOT for

- Career switchers with zero programming background
- “Get rich with AI” seekers
- Teams wanting generic Copilot tips without implementation time

### Competitive wedge vs SoftUni AI

| | SoftUni Vibe Coding | Vibe Coders Academy |
|--|---------------------|---------------------|
| Audience | Beginners, no-code curious | Working developers |
| Tools | Bolt, Lovable, Replit, Firebase Studio | Cursor, MCP, automations |
| Promise | Digital product without writing code | Real codebase + agent workflow |
| Format | Large-scale online academy | Small cohort, founder-led |

*Sources: [SoftUni AI vibe coding](https://ai.softuni.bg/application-vibe-coding), internal research `2026-05-27-bulgaria-ai-coding-market`.*
