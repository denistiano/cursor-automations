# Brand positioning & secret sauce — Bulgaria 2026

**Date:** 2026-06-19  
**Analyst:** Dev Agent (brand sprint)  
**Confidence:** high on competitor positioning; medium on pricing (VCA pre-launch)

## Summary

“Vibe coding” in Bulgaria is **already claimed** by SoftUni AI as **no-code / beginner** software creation via Bolt, Lovable, and Replit ([SoftUni AI program](https://ai.softuni.bg/application-vibe-coding)). Vibe Coders Academy must **not** compete on that definition. The defensible position is **agentic engineering for developers who already code** — Cursor rules, skills, MCP integrations, and automations that ship production repos. Secondary competitors (Telerik GenAI sprint, NobleProg corporate Cursor training) validate demand but lack cohort + capstone + founder-led automation stack positioning.

## Format

- **SoftUni:** Massive online, recorded + live mix, certification path  
- **Telerik / NobleProg:** Short sprints or 1–3 day corporate  
- **VCA (proposed):** 6–8 week cohort, weekly shipped artifacts, small cap, founder in loop

## Strengths vs Vibe Coders Academy

- SoftUni: brand trust, SEO, huge catalog, free funnel  
- Telerik: enterprise credibility  
- NobleProg: fast corporate sales cycle

## Gaps we can exploit

1. **Definition gap:** Own “vibe coders” = devs who *already* code with agents, not no-code makers  
2. **Outcome gap:** Public capstone repo + working automations (this HQ repo as proof)  
3. **Community gap:** Small cohort + founder access vs anonymous MOOC  
4. **Local gap:** Plovdiv/Sofia practitioner community underserved by Sofia-centric brands

## Secret sauce (recommended — Denis confirms day 1)

1. **Curriculum is the product:** Students leave with the same agent stack Denis uses (PM, research, business, social automations)  
2. **Ship weekly:** Non-negotiable artifacts; failure mode is slide-ware  
3. **Honest practitioner brand:** No income guarantees; show real builds including mistakes

## Marketing budget benchmarks (Bulgaria)

- Training Google Ads: ~€3.18 CPA (6.21 BGN) reported for professional courses €1200+ ([Adway case study](https://www.adwaycreative.bg/arlet-k-professional-training-center-case-study-google-ads-strategy/), Dec 2024–Apr 2025)  
- Lean founder-led phase: €50–200/mo tools only until waitlist landing exists  
- Medium: €1–2.5k/mo including part-time ops + modest paid test

## Strategic recommendations

1. **Rename the fight:** Don’t debate “vibe coding” with SoftUni — lead with “Cursor agent engineering for devs who ship.”  
2. **Deck-first + LinkedIn CTA** until landing waitlist is live; FB mirrors announcements only.  
3. **Price premium to free SoftUni intro** but below corporate NobleProg day rates; justify with capstone + automation pack.
